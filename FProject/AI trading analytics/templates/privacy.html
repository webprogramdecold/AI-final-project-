🔒 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

AI Trading Analytics ("we", "our", or "the platform") provides market analytics and AI-powered predictions for educational purposes. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our platform.

By using the platform, you agree to the data practices described in this policy. We may update this policy from time to time; the "Last Updated" date at the top reflects the latest version.

2. Data Sharing & Third Parties

2.1 Exchange APIs

If you link an exchange account, we use your API credentials only to communicate with that exchange on your behalf (e.g. to fetch balances and market data). Your credentials are sent directly to the exchange; we do not share them with any other third parties.

2.2 External Services

The platform may load content or data from external services such as TradingView (charts), CoinMarketCap (market data), and Alternative.me (Fear & Greed index). Those services have their own privacy policies; we encourage you to review them.

2.3 We Do NOT

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Share your API credentials with anyone else
  • Use your data for marketing or advertising
  • Track you across other websites

3. Cookies & Sessions

We use server-side sessions for authentication. A session cookie stores only a session ID (encrypted) and is HttpOnly so it is not accessible via JavaScript. Sessions end when you close the browser or log out.

We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics cookies.

4. Data Retention

Account data is retained until you request deletion. Market and prediction-related data (e.g. price history) may be retained for the operation of the platform. Server logs are typically rotated or deleted after a short period (e.g. 30 days). For any production use, retention practices should comply with applicable laws (e.g. GDPR, CCPA).

5. Children's Privacy

This platform is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, please do not use this platform. Use of crypto and financial services is subject to age restrictions in many jurisdictions.

⚖️ Legal Disclaimer

This Privacy Policy is a template for educational purposes. It is NOT a legally binding document and has NOT been reviewed by legal counsel.

Before deploying this platform for production use:

  • Consult with a qualified attorney specializing in data privacy
  • Ensure compliance with applicable laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
